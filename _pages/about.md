Hi! I am an AI Ph.D. Candidate at Gaoling School of Artificail Intelligence, Renmin University of China, beginning from 2021. My supervisor is Prof. Rui Yan. Prior to RUC, I got my B.S degree in Automation at China University of Petroleum (Beijing) supervised by Prof. Zhu Wang and my M.S degree in Mathematics at China University of Petroleum (Beijing), supervised by Prof. Jianjun Liu.

I have a wide-ranging interest in research topics related to natural language processing and computational social science. My research mainly focuses on Human-centered NLP, exploring how to better understand the capabilities of large language models(LLMs), and their applications in social sciences. This mainly includes using LLMs as agents for computational social science, such as the propagation of fake news, trading in financial markets, and more. It also involves In-context learning of LLMs and knowledge-augmented LLMs. Additionally, I am keen on applying NLP for social good, such as the application of LLMs in advertising and recommendation algorithms, as well as in financial technology. I am also interested in certain training issues of LLMs, such as the problem of continual learning in LLMs. I have published several papers at the top international AI conferences. If you are seeking any form of academic cooperation, please feel free to email me at yuhan.liu@ruc.edu.cn.
